# Population Genetics Coach v1.0.0

Release version based on the clean v0.1 interface and the functional architecture developed through v0.6.

## Curriculum

- Eight lecture-grounded modules matching QBio305 Lectures 1–8.
- Two focused theory extensions derived from Matthew W. Hahn's *Molecular Population Genetics*.
- 26 substantial lessons with topic-specific explanations, equations, worked calculations, interpretation limits, AI transfer tasks, and ten objective questions per lesson.
- Reproducible analysis, Bash/R workflows, research design, writing, and presentation guidance remain in the integrated workspaces rather than appearing as shallow theory modules.

## Assessment

- Every lesson contains five MCQs and five true/false questions.
- The mock exam is progressive and always totals 60 marks.
- Exam questions are drawn only from lecture modules that the learner has started.
- Completing lessons expands and changes the exam paper.
- Exam questions use the mathematical and interpretive pattern of the lecture slides and supplied sample examination.

## Interface

- Removed decorative emoji from learning modules and course content.
- Retained responsive desktop, tablet, and mobile behaviour.
- Retained typed and voice input for AI-supported responses and speech output for AI feedback.
- Retained role-controlled access and app-manager-only source provenance.
