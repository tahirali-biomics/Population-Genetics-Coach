# Population Genetics Coach v0.3.0

This version keeps the clean v0.1 dashboard and adds a complete functional curriculum beta:

- 8 modules and 28 lecture/practical/project-aligned lessons
- substantial structured readings with objectives, equations, worked examples, and common errors
- 10 objective questions per lesson (5 MCQ + 5 true/false)
- AI-evaluated transfer prompt in every lesson
- 7 guided workspaces with 12 or 7 stages
- concept library linked to lesson progress
- local progress and assessment history
- Supabase tables for attempts, workspace progress, and concept mastery
- coordinator dashboard for attempts and milestones

Scientific content is paraphrased from the supplied lecture/practical architecture and should receive final instructor review before graded deployment. Raw teaching files are not included.
